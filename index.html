Welcome To Konark Vaayu

Imagine a landmark where luxury touches the skies and every detail is crafted with timeless elegance. Konark Developers presents the tallest residential tower in Goregaon West, offering stunning 1, 2 & 3 BHK residences with breathtaking city views.
Grand Inception Konark Vaayu Goregaon West
A 51-storey standalone icon rising above SV Road, designed with perfection in every aspect. From spacious vastu-compliant layouts and zero-waste planning to the luxury of residences starting 100 ft. above ground, Konark Vaayu redefines elevated living.
With 9 levels of podium car parking and a dedicated 10th-floor lifestyle zone, residents enjoy world-class indulgences including a clubhouse, gym, and swimming pool. Thoughtfully located in the Western Suburbs, this address ensures seamless access to schools, hospitals, shopping, and entertainment.
Konark Vaayu is more than just a home - it is an exclusive lifestyle crafted for select families who desire the extraordinary.
